# Academicians  
## Adaptive Online Formal Education Platform for Homeschooling

> A low-cost, socially integrated, AI-assisted online education ecosystem designed to combine academic rigor, structured social learning, and accessibility for Indonesia’s next generation of thinkers and leaders.

---

# Overview

Academicians is a research-driven online formal education platform built to address major shortcomings in traditional education and homeschooling systems. The platform combines:

- Structured academic acceleration
- Cohort-based adaptive learning
- AI-assisted tutoring
- Social hierarchy navigation systems
- Collaborative learning environments
- Low-cost accessibility

The project was developed as a multidisciplinary educational technology initiative focused on balancing:
- Intellectual excellence
- Emotional maturity
- Social adaptability
- Cost-efficiency

Academicians is designed primarily for homeschooling students and lower-to-middle income families seeking high-quality formal education without sacrificing structured social interaction.

# Problem Statement

Indonesia’s educational performance continues to face major challenges.

According to OECD PISA 2022 results, Indonesia ranked among the lowest-performing countries in mathematics, reading, and science literacy.

Current educational problems include:

- Over-flexibility without rigorous academic benchmarking
- Lack of competitive intellectual environments
- Homeschooling systems with weak social development
- Expensive high-quality online learning alternatives
- Limited adaptive learning systems for lower-income students

Academicians attempts to solve these issues through an integrated academic and social ecosystem.

# Key Features

## 1. Adaptive Classroom Cohorting

Students are grouped based on:
- Scholastic aptitude
- Personality compatibility
- Predicted relationship dynamics
- Big Five personality traits

This enables:
- Balanced intellectual challenge
- Reduced toxic comparison
- Higher collaborative adaptability
- Improved social growth

---

## 2. Entrance Examination System

The onboarding system consists of:

### Academic Assessment
SAT-style literacy and scholastic competence testing.

### Social Competence Assessment
Big Five psychometric evaluation:
- Openness
- Conscientiousness
- Extraversion
- Agreeableness
- Neuroticism

The system predicts:
- Group compatibility
- Leadership dynamics
- Conflict adaptation potential
- Growth-oriented cohort structures

---

## 3. AI Chatbot Teacher System

Every classroom includes an AI-assisted tutor capable of:

- 24/7 question answering
- Personalized learning assistance
- Sentiment analysis
- Emotional response recognition
- Adaptive teaching strategies

### AI Features
- Facial recognition sentiment analysis
- Audio-based frustration detection
- Learning pattern adaptation
- Cohort-level optimization

---

## 4. Discord-Inspired Social Infrastructure

Students can:
- Create clubs and communities
- Join peer learning groups
- Access discovery pages
- Participate in collaborative assignments
- Build social and leadership skills

Features include:
- Voice channels
- Text channels
- Role hierarchies
- Moderation systems
- Community leaderboards

---

## 5. Tuition Reduction System

Academicians implements performance-based tuition fee reductions.

The reduction system considers:
- Academic growth
- Collaborative behavior
- Social contribution
- Consistency of improvement

### Formula Concept

```math
Reduction Percentage = ΔPerformance / Session Count
```
